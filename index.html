About Sam

Human Capital
Development Specialist.

Sam Rodrigues is a Forbes Coaches Council Member Leader and one of the most active Portuguese-speaking consultants in human capital development — operating across Africa, Europe, and the Lusophone world. Affiliated with Baxtter Corporation as Senior Consultant and Executive Coach.

His programmes have touched organisations across Angola, Mozambique, Portugal, Cape Verde, São Tomé e Príncipe, and Guinea-Bissau — combining corporate training, executive coaching, strategic consulting, and people development.
